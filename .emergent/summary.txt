<product_requirements>
The Job Rocket application aims to be a comprehensive platform connecting job seekers, recruiters, and administrators, featuring an Applicant Tracking System (ATS), subscription models for job listings and CV search, and payment processing via Payfast.

**Core Features & Implemented State:**
1.  **UI & Theme:** Modern techie, rocket feel UI with dark greys, blues, gradients, glassmorphism, and animations.
2.  **Gamified Onboarding:** Full registration/login for Job Seekers (profile completion with points, visual trophy progress) and Recruiters (company profile completion).
3.  **Company Structure & Team Management:** Recruiters can manage main company, branches, and invite team members (email deferral).
4.  **Job Listing & Posting:** Recruiters can post jobs (single/bulk), with mandatory fields (Title, Location, Salary, Type, Work Type, Industry, Description) and optional ones. Jobs auto-expire in 35 days, are archived, and can be reposted.
5.  **Job Application System:** Easy Apply modal pre-populates job seeker data (name, email, CV, skills, optional cover letter). Job seekers track applications, recruiters manage them.
6.  **Pricing & Payment Packages:** Defined packages for job listings and CV search are displayed.
7.  **Payfast Integration (Completed):** Full backend integration of Payfast, including automated package activation upon successful payment and sandbox mode configuration for testing.
8.  **Admin Discount Codes (Completed):** Backend and frontend implementation for administrators to create, manage (view, edit, deactivate), and validate discount codes (percentage or fixed value).
9.  **Enhanced Navigation (Completed):** Admin users can now seamlessly navigate between the main job platform and the admin dashboard without being trapped.
10. **Improved Job Listings Display (Completed):** Job data model fixes, expanded dummy job data (now 20 jobs), and resolution of frontend display issues including correct rendering of job cards, salary, and company info.
11. **Job Details Modal (Completed):** Clicking job titles now opens a comprehensive job details modal for enhanced viewing.
12. **UI/UX Fixes (Completed):** Resolved issues with Easy Apply button spacing (found to be correct, but fixed modal opening), job title click behavior, and general rendering errors like the  import issue.

**Current focus of work:** Make cover letter mandatory for job applications and implement CV document upload functionality.
