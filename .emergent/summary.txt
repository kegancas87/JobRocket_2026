<product_requirements>
The Job Rocket application aims to be a full-stack platform connecting job seekers, recruiters/employers, and administrators. Its core features include a built-in Applicant Tracking System (ATS), a subscription model for unlimited job listings, and CV search for headhunting, with recruiters paying for services and job seekers having free access, utilizing Payfast for payments.

So far, the application has:
1.  **Job Listing UI**: An initial modern UI for displaying job listings, matching a provided screenshot.
2.  **Tech-Rocket Theme**: The UI was enhanced with a techie, rocket feel using light/dark greys and blues, incorporating gradients, glassmorphism, and subtle animations.
3.  **Job Seeker Gamified Onboarding**: A complete registration and login system for job seekers. This includes a gamified profile completion process with points (100 total): Profile Picture (5), About Me (10), Work History (10), Skills (20), Education with document upload (10), Achievements (10), Intro Video (20), Apply to 5 Jobs (10), Email Job Alert (5). A trophy icon with a circular progress indicator turns gold with a rocket launch animation upon 100 points.
4.  **Recruiter/Employer Onboarding**: Users can choose their role (job seeker/recruiter) at registration, and the system remembers it. Recruiters also have a gamified onboarding for company profile completion (Company Logo, Cover Image, About Company, Number of Employees, Website Link, LinkedIn Link), with points divided appropriately.
5.  **Company Structure (In Progress)**: Recruiters need to add a main company, with the ability to add branches (optional location/email) and invite team members, associating them with one or many branches.
</product_requirements>
